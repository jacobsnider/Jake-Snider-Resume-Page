ABOUT

Jake started playing music at a young age, beginning with a brief stint of piano lessons from his mother as a child. He played trumpet all throughout middle school but his true musical passion arises behind a drum kit. He's been a part of Louisville's DIY punk/hardcore community for over a decade. This page is a compendium of his musical career.

MOUNTAIN ASLEEP

Possibly Jake's most noteworthy band. Mountain Asleep was an unrelenting force with Jake as the lead vocalist and lyricist.
